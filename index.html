🚀 The Beginning

My investment journey started in 2019 with a simple question: "How do I make my money work for me?" That curiosity led me deep into the world of financial markets, from reading Benjamin Graham to analyzing quarterly earnings late into the night.

📚 Philosophy

I believe in the power of compound interest and the importance of patience. I don't chase hot tips or meme stocks — I invest in businesses I understand, at prices that make sense, and hold them through market cycles.

"
The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient.
— Warren Buffett

🎯 What Drives Me

Beyond returns, I'm fascinated by how businesses create value. Understanding a company's strategy, culture, and competitive dynamics is as rewarding as watching the portfolio grow. Every investment tells a story — I make sure I know it well before I commit.
